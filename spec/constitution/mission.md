# Mission

## Product Name
Demo Day Room

## Mission Statement
Demo Day Room is a focused rehearsal system for hackathon founders. It turns a rough project into a pressure-tested pitch by guiding founders through a structured loop: prepare context, record a pitch, survive adversarial AI judge Q&A, and receive a structured post-mortem debrief.

## Core Problem
Technical hackathon founders ship fast but pitch poorly. They lose not because of bad ideas, but because they freeze under adversarial questioning, bury conclusions, speak too fast, and fail to connect their solution to judge priorities. No existing tool simulates a realistic 3-minute investor room for free in a 48-hour window.

## Core User
A hackathon founder who has already built something and now needs to:
- Clarify the idea into a coherent narrative
- Pressure-test it against adversarial judge questions
- Understand exactly what broke and how to fix it before the real presentation

## Core Value Proposition
Not a pitch generator. A pressure-testing system. The app rehearses demo day in a realistic workflow — capture context, record pitch, face AI judges live, receive a structured fracture map, continue with a post-mortem coach.

## Product Principles
1. **The workflow is narrow and opinionated.** Brief → Room → Debrief. Not an open workspace.
2. **The Room is the emotional center.** High-stakes, focused, slightly adversarial.
3. **The Debrief is the hero artifact.** It is what the founder pays attention to.
4. **Don't make users do the AI's job.** Founders dump raw context. AI extracts structure silently.
5. **One dominant next action per screen.** Always move toward a stronger next attempt.
6. **Institutional, not flashy.** Feels like a private review room, not a generic chatbot.

## Non-Goals
This product is NOT:
- A pitch generator or slide maker
- A CRM or investor database
- A startup planning suite
- A general AI note-taking app
- A broad analytics dashboard
- A mobile app (desktop web only, MVP)
